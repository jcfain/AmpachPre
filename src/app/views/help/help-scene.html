Keyboard Short Cuts
Key Action
"." skip ahead 10 secs
"@" skip back 10 secs
"space" Pause
"shift" Previous Track
"sym" Next Track
"c" or "s" Stops audio, Clears Now Playing list
"n" View Now Playing
"l" View Now Playing List
"h" Home
"?" This scene


Building a playlist:
You can build a playlist either by swiping a list Item on almost any scene. (artist, album, Songs, etc)
You can also tap the on the list item you want to add to the playlist.
If you exit AmpachPre all temporary playlists will be deleted.


Saving a playlist:
Work hard on building a playlist? You can save it by first going back to the home scene either by tapping the home icon or using the keyboard shortcut "h".
Tap Saved Playlists from the home scene.
Tap "add" to make a new container for your play list.
Create a name(required) for the playlist and add notes if you wish.
Select your newly created playlist container to enter it.
Tap the icon to save the playlist.


Downloading a playlist:
Going some where where no internet is available?
Downloading a playlist is exactly like "Saving a playlist".
If not there already, go back to the home scene either by tapping the home icon or using the keyboard shortcut "h".
Tap "Downloads" from the home scene.
Tap "add" to make a new container for your Downloaded playlist.
Create a name(required) for the playlist and add notes if you wish.
Select your newly created Downloaded playlist container to enter it.
Tap the icon to begin downloading.


A few notes on downloading:
When you begin downloading, after the first song is done you may tap on it to begin listening.
if you back gesture from the download scene, change account or go back to search the download will be canceled.
until you visit the downloading scene again and restart the downloads.
If you exit AmpachPre all temporary playlists will be deleted.


Adding bookmarks:
You can bookmark a Artist, Album or Song if you tend to revisit it often.
Tap the of any Artist, Album or Song and tap "Bookmark".
From the "Home" scene tap "Bookmarks" to retrieve them.

You can also bookmark Songs and certain positions within the song
from either Now Playing scene's APP menu.


Albums of the Moment:
Don't know what you want to listen to?
Albums of the Moment will pull 12 random albums down.
You can refresh this as many times as you like
until you find your current mood.


Searching:
You can begin a search two different ways.
The first: simply start typing from the home scene.
Second: Tap the search menu List item to open the search field.
After the search field is open, tap the arrow below it to change the category if needed.
Default is "Search All".


Home Scene List:
You can re arange the home list any way you like. Delete the ones you dont use.
Reset the home scene at any time by visiting the Application menu and tapping "Reset Home Scene".
